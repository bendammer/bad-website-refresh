ABOUT

ben is a 21 year old freelance photographer based in neuss, germany. in 2015 he held his first camera and couldn't let go since. his passion with photography is mainly about portrait, fashion and street photography. in 2020 he started developing his own brand 'BAD PHOTOGRAPHY', which led him to his first commercial jobs in the gastronomy, fashion and industry sector.
the brands name has it's origin in his names initials Ben Aaron Dammer.
despite taking pictures he studies communication and multimediamanagement in düsseldorf and will complete his bachelors degree in 2023.
on this self-written website he displays his general portfolio and latest work.
